My top 4 games

League Of Legends

League of Legends is a popular online multiplayer video game developed and published by Riot Games. It is a MOBA (multiplayer online battle arena) game where players control champions with unique abilities and battle against a team of other players in a strategic, objective-based gameplay. The game has gained a massive following worldwide, with professional leagues and tournaments held regularly, making it one of the biggest esports games in the world.

Valorant

Valorant is a popular online multiplayer first-person shooter game developed and published by Riot Games. It is a tactical shooter game where players select a unique agent with special abilities and work together in a team to complete objectives and eliminate the opposing team. The game has gained popularity in the esports scene, with tournaments and leagues held regularly, and has gained a reputation for its competitive gameplay, high skill ceiling, and strategic depth.

Minecraft

Minecraft is a popular sandbox video game developed and published by Mojang Studios. In Minecraft, players explore a procedurally generated 3D world, where they can gather resources, build structures, and craft tools and items. The game has multiple modes, including survival, creative, and adventure modes, and supports both single-player and multiplayer gameplay. Minecraft has gained immense popularity worldwide, with a massive following among both casual and hardcore gamers, and has become a cultural phenomenon, inspiring various spin-off games, merchandise, and even an educational version of the game.

Fortnite

Fortnite is a popular online multiplayer video game developed and published by Epic Games. It is a battle royale game, where up to 100 players fight to be the last one standing on a shrinking map, while also scavenging for weapons and resources to help them survive. The game also has other modes, such as creative mode and save the world mode, which offer different gameplay experiences. Fortnite has gained immense popularity worldwide, particularly among younger gamers, and has become a cultural phenomenon, inspiring various merchandise, dances, and even a popular concert series held within the game.
