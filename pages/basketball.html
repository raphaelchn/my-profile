My top 4 players

Luka Doncic

Luka Doncic is a Slovenian professional basketball player who currently plays for the Dallas Mavericks in the NBA, known for his impressive skills, versatility, and basketball IQ at a young age.

Michael Jordan

Michael Jordan is a retired American professional basketball player widely regarded as one of the greatest basketball players of all time, known for his exceptional athleticism, scoring ability, and competitive drive.

Lebron James

LeBron James is an American professional basketball player who currently plays for the Los Angeles Lakers in the NBA, known for his all-around skills, athleticism, and leadership on and off the court. He is widely considered one of the greatest basketball players of all time.

Stephen Curry

Steph Curry is an American professional basketball player who currently plays for the Golden State Warriors in the NBA, known for his incredible shooting range, ball-handling skills, and ability to make difficult shots. He is widely regarded as one of the best shooters in NBA history and has revolutionized the game with his style of play.
